About us

We at Crecer our expertise is vast in Design Consultation, Project Management & Facility Management.

Crecer building services

Crecer Building Services was founded in 2016, to provide world- class building services and facility maintenance solutions for the rising portfolio of high-end residential and commercial real estate across Nigeria. Our goal is to deploy our resources to ensuring our clients enjoy maximum utility, while preserving the functional, aesthetic and economic value of their facilities.

The organization has grown since inception, and now manages NGN 1 Billion of real estate assets in Lagos, ,Port Harcourt and Abuja, and cutting across different customer segments.

As a registered members of the International Facility Management Association, having years of experience in the building construction and facility maintenance industry, and with a team of experts in our 3 offices around Nigeria, we work to provide seamless and cost-effective facility maintenance solutions that give our clients the critical advantage with the maintenance of their buildings, equipment & critical infrastructure, hence improving facility utility and return on facility-focused investments.
